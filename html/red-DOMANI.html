(RED)REDESIGN

Bono and Bobby Shriver founded (RED) with a simple mission, to make it easy for people and businesses to join the fight against AIDS. Since its inception, (RED) has raised over $360 million with the help of their partners, paving the way for an AIDS free generation.

Now a well-established organization, (RED) enlisted the help of DOMANI to refresh their website to focus on three key areas: strong product pages and purchase action; making the charitable impact of (RED) more palpable to consumers; and building a scalable and easy to manage platform that could support (RED)'s expanding impact.

JOINING THE FIGHT AGAINST AIDS
In order to build upon (RED)'s unique value, DOMANI led the strategy, UX, visual design, and development of the organization's refreshed digital platform.

By auditing and consolidating content, DOMANI re-architected the site - successfully
heroing products with clear calls-to-action,while simultaneously emphasizing how (RED)
functions and meaningfully leverages charitable contributions.

Launched in time for World AIDS Day 2016, the new website garnered significant attention
through (RED)’s celebrity affiliations, prime time TV spots,iTunes buyout, and pop up kiosks
around the country.

Due to (RED)’s partnership with Apple, the new (RED) website was one of the first charity
sites to feature Apple Pay via Stripe payment services. This feature was available to DOMANI
in an early release, and was launched on the red.org donation page.
