DESIGNING THE DESTINATION AUTHORITY

Whether browsing for inspiration, or quickly pinpointing a specific destination through predictive search and interactive maps, The Luxury Collection Destination site helps travelers make the most of their journey. The responsive, mobile-friendly platform also features an integrated booking solution and a scalable, content-managed framework that facilitates localization in 9+ languages.

DOMANI led all aspects of this global brand initiative including strategy, content development, IA/UX, design, and development. We also continue to work closely with the global brand team to provide post-launch analytics and performance marketing support.

-Quick booking CTAs integrated across the entire experience

-A comprehensive analytics suite tracks regional performance, which can be attributed back to individual property bookings

-Sort destinations by preferences and/or geography

When guests at Luxury Collection properties connect to their in-room WiFi they are presented with a local area content pulled from the platform's CMS.
