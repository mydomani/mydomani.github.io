The White House


United State of Women.


DOMANI had the immense honor of partnering with BBH LA to design and develop the United State of Women's digital platform.
Convened by the White House, the Summit rallied all of us together to celebrate what we’ve achieved, and how The United State of Women and our allies are going to take action moving forward.
The platform served not only to promote the June 14, 2016 USOW Summit held by the White House, but also to celebrate women, applaud their achievements and support their dreams well beyond the Summit event.

Today we'll Change Tomorrow..



In the lead-up to the USOW Summit, the site's focus was on highlighting the key gender equality issues that would be the focus of a series of Solutions Seminars.



By celebrating all the work that's being done by and for women and girls in the United States and bolstering initiatives that make a difference in our collective future, the site will live on well beyond The Summit as a historical record of the power of the United State of Women.
-Clean, simplistic design that lets the cause shine


-Social integration gives everyone a voice


-A lasting platform supporting a universal movement.
